Lifestyle

A peek into the lifestyle of the Zermattians

Zermatt is an upper class tourist resort. It is a pretty, well organized, village in the Alps. It can be reached by train. Cars are not allowed.

A fantastic place for a holiday for hikers in Summer and Skiers in Winter.

Despite the huge number of visitors here, the locals remain true to themselves and their habits. They don't learn anything from foreigners. Every citizen is proud of their small homeland and the country as a whole.
